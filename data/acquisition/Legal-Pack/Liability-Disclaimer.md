## Part 2: AI Output Disclaimer

NutriAID's AI outputs are generated by large language models and validated by an automated self-healing supervision pipeline. Despite this supervision:

- AI outputs may contain inaccuracies or errors not detected by the validation pipeline
- Nutritional values and recipe estimates are approximations and may not reflect actual product composition
- The AI system does not have access to real-time food safety recall data
- AI recommendations are based on declared user data; undisclosed conditions or medications may affect their safety or relevance
- The AI recipe generator produces plausible but algorithmically generated recipes; results should be verified against established culinary and nutritional standards

**The operator (platform owner) accepts responsibility for ensuring that the platform is deployed appropriately and that end users are informed of these limitations.**

## Part 5: GDPR and Data Protection

### Data Controller Responsibility

Upon acquisition of the NutriAID platform, the Buyer becomes the data controller for all user data processed by the platform. The Buyer assumes full responsibility for compliance with:

- EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) 2016/679
- Applicable national data protection laws in all jurisdictions of operation
- Any additional sector-specific regulations applicable to health and food intolerance data

The Seller represents that the platform has been developed with GDPR compliance in mind, including:

- Lawful basis for data processing documented in the privacy policy (consent, contract, legitimate interest, legal obligation)
- Data minimisation principles applied to user profile collection
- Explicit consent mechanism for health/intolerance data (Art. 9 GDPR special categories)
- Right to erasure implemented: account deletion removes all personal data within 30 days
- Data portability implemented: users can export all their data as JSON from the dashboard
- Newsletter consent recorded with timestamp and source for full audit trail
- Appropriate disclosure of all third-party processors in the privacy policy

### Health Data Classification

Food intolerance and allergy data qualifies as health data under GDPR Article 9 (special category data). The platform collects this data on the basis of explicit user consent. The Buyer must ensure an appropriate legal basis for processing this data is established and documented in the platform's privacy policy for any new jurisdiction of operation.

### TikTok Pixel and International Data Transfers

The Platform includes a TikTok Pixel integration for marketing analytics. This integration:

- Is activated only after explicit user consent via the cookie consent mechanism
- Transfers data to TikTok's servers, which may be located outside the EEA (including USA and Singapore)
- Is disclosed in the platform's privacy policy as a third-party processor with potential data transfer outside the EEA

The Buyer must ensure that the TikTok Pixel integration continues to comply with applicable data protection law, particularly regarding cross-border data transfers. If the Buyer operates in jurisdictions with stricter requirements, the integration may need to be reviewed or disabled.

## Part 6: Regulatory Compliance — EU AI Act

NutriAID has been designed with awareness of the EU Artificial Intelligence Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689). The platform:

- Does not make autonomous decisions with direct legal effects on individuals
- Always presents AI outputs as informational and non-binding
- Enforces mandatory medical and allergy disclaimers on all safety-sensitive AI outputs via the Medical Safety Worker
- Provides human-in-the-loop operation (users can disregard AI recommendations)
- Maintains AI execution logs for transparency and auditability (visible in Admin AI Test Lab)
- Supports multi-model configuration, allowing the operator to select AI providers that comply with applicable AI regulation

The Buyer should consult legal counsel to assess whether any specific deployment scenario creates obligations under the EU AI Act or other applicable AI regulation.
