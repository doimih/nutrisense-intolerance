## Recitals

WHEREAS, the Transferor has developed and owns the NutriAID platform, including all associated intellectual property as defined herein;

WHEREAS, the Transferee desires to acquire all such intellectual property from the Transferor;

WHEREAS, the Transferor desires to transfer all such intellectual property to the Transferee on the terms set forth in this Agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and the purchase price set forth herein, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. Definitions

**"Platform"** means the NutriAID software platform, comprising the frontend application (Next.js 14), the backend admin console (Next.js 15), all AI orchestration code, all worker definitions, all validation and correction systems, all database schemas and migration files, all configuration files, and all associated infrastructure code.

**"Intellectual Property"** means all of the following owned by or licensed to the Transferor in connection with the Platform:

a) **Source Code:** All source code, object code, compiled code, scripts, and associated files constituting the Platform, including but not limited to frontend, backend, AI workers, validators, and auto-correctors;

b) **Documentation:** All technical documentation, API documentation, user guides, deployment guides, architecture diagrams, and training materials relating to the Platform;

c) **Domain Names:** The domain name `nutriaid.eu` and all associated subdomains, and any other domain names registered by the Transferor in connection with the Platform;

d) **Trademarks and Brand Assets:** The "NutriAID" name and logo, all associated stylistic elements, brand guidelines, and marketing materials;

e) **Data:** All anonymised user data, intolerance-ingredient mapping datasets, and any other proprietary datasets developed in connection with the Platform, to the extent transferable under applicable data protection law;

f) **Third-Party Licences:** All transferable licences, API keys, and service agreements related to the operation of the Platform;

g) **Copyrights:** All copyrights in the source code, documentation, content, and other materials constituting the Platform, including the right to prepare derivative works.

## 2. Transfer

### 2.1 Assignment

Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement and upon receipt of the Purchase Price, the Transferor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Transferee all right, title, and interest in and to the Intellectual Property, free and clear of all liens, encumbrances, and third-party claims.

### 2.2 Effective Date

The transfer of Intellectual Property shall be effective upon the Transferee's confirmation of receipt of all deliverables specified in Section 4 and payment of the full Purchase Price.

## 4. Deliverables

The Transferor shall deliver the following to the Transferee within 14 calendar days of signing:

a) Complete source code via private Git repository transfer or ZIP archive;

b) All environment variable templates and configuration documentation;

c) All database migration files and schema documentation;

d) Transfer of domain name(s) to the Transferee's registrar account;

e) Transfer of all third-party service accounts (Stripe, SendGrid, etc.) or provision of all API keys and credentials;

f) All design assets (logo files, brand assets in original formats);

g) Written confirmation of deletion of all copies retained by the Transferor.

---

## 5. Transition Support

The Transferor agrees to provide transition support for a period of **30 (thirty) calendar days** following the Effective Date, including:

- Up to 10 hours of technical consultation (video call or email)
- Assistance with deployment and environment configuration
- Answers to questions regarding the codebase, AI configuration, and admin console

Additional transition support beyond this period may be arranged by separate agreement.

---

## 6. Representations and Warranties

The Transferor represents and warrants that:

a) The Transferor is the sole and exclusive owner of the Intellectual Property and has the full right and authority to transfer it;

b) The Intellectual Property is free and clear of all liens, encumbrances, and third-party claims;

c) The source code does not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights;

d) The Platform does not contain any undisclosed malware, backdoors, or intentional defects;

e) All third-party software components used in the Platform are used in compliance with their respective licences;

f) The Platform has been developed in compliance with applicable EU data protection law (GDPR).

---

## 7. Post-Transfer Obligations

After the Effective Date, the Transferor agrees to:

a) Permanently delete all copies of the source code and Intellectual Property from Transferor-controlled systems;

b) Not use the "NutriAID" brand, name, or any confusingly similar name in any commercial activity;

c) Not develop or deploy any substantially similar AI nutrition platform for a period of **24 (twenty-four) months** from the Effective Date, within the European Union.

---

## 8. Limitation of Liability

The Transferor's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the Purchase Price. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

---

## 9. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Romania. Disputes shall be submitted to the courts of Bucharest.
