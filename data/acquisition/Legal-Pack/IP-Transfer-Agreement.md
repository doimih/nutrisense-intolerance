## 1. Definitions

**"Platform"** means the NutriAID software platform in its entirety as of the Effective Date, comprising:

- The **frontend application** (Next.js 14) including all pages, components, and user-facing features: onboarding modal, dashboard (guidance, journal, history, recipes, profile, GDPR), PWA manifest and service worker, early adopter programme, AI guidance request and regeneration flow, PDF export for individual guidance entries, history viewer, recipe module (RecipeModal, CookingMode), shopping list, progress tracking, newsletter opt-in (footer form and post-registration popup), TikTok Pixel integration, and pricing and billing pages (plan selection, checkout, cancellation);
- The **backend admin console** (Next.js 15) including the superadmin panel, all admin settings (AI Brain, Stripe, SMTP/Brevo, reCAPTCHA, 2FA, PWA, TikTok Pixel configuration, backup scheduler), the AI Test Lab, and all superadmin API endpoints;
- The **AI orchestration system**: intent detection engine, 10 specialised AI workers (Profile Analyzer, Intolerance Checker, Allergy Checker, Meal Plan Generator, Recipe Builder, Nutrition Calculator, Medical Safety, Supplement Advisor, Progress Tracker, Shopping List), the Worker Supervisor self-healing pipeline (schema validation → semantic validation → safety checks → auto-correction → re-validation), and the GEO/diversity/behavioural engine;
- All **database schemas and migration files** (PostgreSQL + Drizzle ORM), covering 11 tables: users, verificationTokens, subscriptions, userProfiles, monitoringEntries, userProblems, passwordResetTokens, guidanceHistory, recipes, recipeBatches, recipeUsage;
- All **configuration files**, Docker Compose setup, Traefik reverse proxy configuration, environment variable templates, and infrastructure-as-code;
- All **associated content and documentation** included in the Acquisition Portal.

**"Intellectual Property"** means all of the following owned by or licensed to the Transferor in connection with the Platform:

a) **Source Code:** All source code, object code, compiled code, scripts, and associated files constituting the Platform, including but not limited to frontend, backend, AI workers, validators, auto-correctors, TikTok Pixel integration, PWA implementation, Brevo newsletter system, and billing integration;

b) **Documentation:** All technical documentation, API documentation, user guides, deployment guides, architecture diagrams, acquisition portal content, and all materials included in the Acquisition Portal data room;

c) **Domain Names:** The domain name `nutriaid.eu` and all associated subdomains (including `nutriaid-intolerances.ro` if registered), and any other domain names registered by the Transferor in connection with the Platform;

d) **Trademarks and Brand Assets:** The "NutriAID" name and logo, all associated stylistic elements, brand guidelines, colour system, typography specifications, and marketing materials;

e) **Data:** All anonymised user data, intolerance-ingredient mapping datasets, recipe datasets generated by the platform, and any other proprietary datasets developed in connection with the Platform, to the extent transferable under applicable data protection law (GDPR);

f) **Third-Party Service Accounts:** All transferable licences, API keys, and service agreements related to the operation of the Platform, including but not limited to: Stripe (payment processing), Brevo/Sendinblue (newsletter and transactional email), OpenAI or compatible AI API accounts, Google reCAPTCHA, TikTok Pixel account, S3-compatible backup storage, and hosting/server accounts;

g) **Copyrights:** All copyrights in the source code, documentation, content, and other materials constituting the Platform, including the right to prepare derivative works.

## 2. Transfer

### 2.1 Assignment

Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement and upon receipt of the Purchase Price, the Transferor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Transferee all right, title, and interest in and to the Intellectual Property, free and clear of all liens, encumbrances, and third-party claims.

### 2.2 Effective Date

The transfer of Intellectual Property shall be effective upon the Transferee's confirmation of receipt of all deliverables specified in Section 4 and payment of the full Purchase Price.

## 4. Deliverables

The Transferor shall deliver the following to the Transferee within 14 calendar days of signing:

a) Complete source code via private Git repository transfer or encrypted ZIP archive;

b) All environment variable templates (`.env.example` files) and configuration documentation;

c) All database migration files, Drizzle ORM schema, and schema documentation;

d) Transfer of domain name(s) to the Transferee's registrar account;

e) Transfer of all third-party service accounts or provision of all API keys and credentials, including: Stripe (payment processor), Brevo (newsletter and transactional email), OpenAI or compatible AI API, Google reCAPTCHA, TikTok Pixel, S3-compatible backup storage, and hosting/server accounts;

f) All design assets (logo files, brand assets in Figma, SVG, and PNG formats; brand guidelines);

g) Written confirmation of permanent deletion of all copies retained by the Transferor.

## 5. Transition Support

The Transferor agrees to provide transition support for a period of **30 (thirty) calendar days** following the Effective Date, including:

- Up to 10 hours of technical consultation (video call or email)
- Assistance with deployment and environment configuration (Docker, Traefik, environment variables)
- Answers to questions regarding the codebase, AI orchestrator configuration, admin console, Brevo/newsletter setup, TikTok Pixel configuration, and Stripe webhook registration

Additional transition support beyond this period may be arranged by separate agreement.

## 6. Representations and Warranties

The Transferor represents and warrants that:

a) The Transferor is the sole and exclusive owner of the Intellectual Property and has the full right and authority to transfer it;

b) The Intellectual Property is free and clear of all liens, encumbrances, and third-party claims;

c) The source code does not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights;

d) The Platform does not contain any undisclosed malware, backdoors, or intentional defects;

e) All third-party software components used in the Platform are used in compliance with their respective licences (MIT, Apache 2.0, and similar permissive licences);

f) The Platform has been developed with GDPR compliance in mind, including lawful basis documentation, data minimisation, right to erasure implementation, GDPR-compliant newsletter consent recording, and appropriate disclosure of third-party processors (Stripe, Brevo, Google reCAPTCHA, TikTok Pixel) in the privacy policy;

g) The TikTok Pixel integration is implemented in a manner that requires user consent prior to activation, consistent with GDPR requirements for marketing tracking technologies.

---

## 7. Post-Transfer Obligations

After the Effective Date, the Transferor agrees to:

a) Permanently delete all copies of the source code and Intellectual Property from Transferor-controlled systems within 14 days of transfer;

b) Not use the "NutriAID" brand, name, or any confusingly similar name in any commercial activity;

c) Not develop or deploy any substantially similar AI-powered nutrition intolerance management platform for a period of **24 (twenty-four) months** from the Effective Date, within the European Union.

---

## 8. Limitation of Liability

The Transferor's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the Purchase Price. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.
