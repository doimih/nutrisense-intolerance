## Production Notes

**Format:** Social media vertical (9:16) or square (1:1)  
**Duration:** 28–32 seconds  
**Platform targets:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts  
**Tone:** Fast, clear, problem–solution  
**Style:** Screen recording with captions + upbeat background music
