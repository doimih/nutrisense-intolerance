### Medium Description (150 words)

NutriAID is a next-generation nutrition SaaS platform that combines artificial intelligence with deep personalisation to serve users managing food intolerances, allergies, and complex dietary needs.

At its core, NutriAID uses a patented-pending AI orchestration system with 10 specialised workers — from profile analysis and intolerance checking, to meal plan generation and medical safety review — each supervised by an automatic validation and self-correction layer. This means NutriAID never delivers unsafe or structurally invalid AI outputs.

Built for the European market with full i18n (Romanian and English), GDPR-native architecture, Stripe billing, and a complete admin console, NutriAID is ready for immediate deployment as a white-label product for clinics, wellness brands, or direct-to-consumer platforms.

### Long Description (300 words)

NutriAID is a production-ready AI nutrition SaaS platform purpose-built for users navigating food intolerances, allergies, and complex dietary restrictions. Unlike generic nutrition apps that apply one-size-fits-all recommendations, NutriAID's AI engine is specifically engineered to understand and enforce each user's unique dietary profile at every step of the guidance process.

The platform's AI Brain is a 10-worker orchestration system that routes every user request through a structured pipeline: first analysing the user's profile, then checking for intolerance and allergen conflicts, then generating personalised meal plans or recipes, then calculating nutritional values, and finally passing all content through a medical safety reviewer that ensures no diagnosis language, no prescription advice, and no absolute claims reach the user.

Every worker output is validated by an automatic Supervisor — checking schema integrity and semantic safety — and corrected by an Auto-Corrector if needed, using either a primary AI model, a fallback model, or a $0-cost rule-based engine. The system is guaranteed to produce a valid, safe output even during full AI API outages.

For operators, NutriAID provides a fully featured admin console built on Next.js 15, with AI Brain configuration, user management, security event logging, audit trails, and a complete acquisition portal with download tracking. All AI behaviour is configurable at runtime — no code changes, no restarts.

NutriAID is deployed with Docker and Traefik, runs on any VPS at approximately €17/month for the current scale, and is ready for horizontal scaling to 50,000+ monthly active users.

Currently offered for acquisition at €70,000–€120,000, NutriAID represents an exceptional opportunity for a health tech operator, digital agency, or strategic acquirer seeking a technically differentiated, EU-native, white-label-ready AI nutrition platform.
