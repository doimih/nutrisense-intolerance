## Production Notes

**Format:** Screen recording + voiceover  
**Duration:** 4–6 minutes  
**Audience:** Prospective acquirers, investors, B2B partners  
**Tone:** Calm, confident, enterprise-grade  
**Resolution:** 1920×1080, 30fps minimum  
**Narration:** Professional male or female voiceover, neutral EU English accent  

---

## Script

---

### [SCENE 1 — TITLE CARD — 0:00–0:12]

*Black screen. NutriAID logo fades in. Tagline appears below.*

**VOICEOVER:**
"NutriAID. AI-powered nutrition, built for people who need it to be safe."

*Logo fades out. Cut to landing page.*

---

### [SCENE 2 — LANDING PAGE — 0:12–0:45]

*Browser opens to nutriaid.eu. Scroll slowly through the landing page.*

**VOICEOVER:**
"NutriAID is a nutrition SaaS platform designed for users managing food intolerances, allergies, and complex dietary restrictions. It delivers personalised meal plans, recipes, supplement guidance, and symptom tracking — powered by an orchestrated AI pipeline that automatically enforces each user's dietary profile."

*Pause on the feature section showing the AI brain diagram.*

"Unlike generic nutrition apps, NutriAID's AI doesn't just suggest meals. It validates every output against the user's intolerances and allergies — and corrects itself automatically if anything goes wrong."

---

### [SCENE 3 — USER REGISTRATION & ONBOARDING — 0:45–1:30]

*Click 'Get Started'. Registration form appears.*

**VOICEOVER:**
"The onboarding flow collects the information the AI needs to personalise every response."

*Fill in: name, email, password. Continue to profile setup.*

"Users define their dietary type — omnivore, vegetarian, vegan, and others..."

*Select 'Omnivore'. Move to intolerance selector.*

"...declare any food intolerances..."

*Select: Gluten, Lactose.*

"...and allergies."

*Select: Peanuts.*

"This profile is then used by every AI worker in every subsequent request."

*Onboarding complete. Dashboard appears.*

---

### [SCENE 4 — AI GUIDANCE — 1:30–3:00]

*Click on 'AI Guidance'. Chat interface opens.*

**VOICEOVER:**
"Here's the AI guidance interface. Let's send a real request."

*Type: "Create a 5-day meal plan for me, avoiding gluten, lactose, and high-FODMAP foods."*

*Press send. AI thinking indicator appears.*

"Behind the scenes, NutriAID has just triggered a 6-worker AI pipeline."

*Screen splits to show animated worker chain running.*

"The profile analyzer reads the user's declared profile. The intolerance checker flags incompatible ingredients. The allergy checker validates allergen safety. The meal plan generator creates a complete, personalised 5-day plan — using only safe ingredients. The nutrition calculator estimates macro and calorie values. And the medical safety worker performs a final review, adding a required medical disclaimer."

*AI response appears in the chat.*

"Every step is supervised. Every output is validated. If any worker produces an invalid or unsafe response, the system automatically corrects it — without the user ever knowing."

*Scroll through the full meal plan response.*

"The result: a complete, intolerance-safe, nutritionally balanced meal plan. In under 10 seconds."

---

### [SCENE 5 — MONITORING JOURNAL — 3:00–3:45]

*Navigate to the Monitoring Journal section.*

**VOICEOVER:**
"NutriAID's monitoring journal allows users to track what they eat, how they feel, and what symptoms they experience — day by day."

*Open a journal entry. Show food tags, symptom input, wellbeing score slider.*

"The AI progress tracking worker analyses journal history to identify patterns — which foods correlate with symptoms, which days show improvement, and what changes may be making a difference."

*Show the weekly AI summary card.*

"This turns passive logging into actionable insight."

---

### [SCENE 6 — ADMIN CONSOLE — 3:45–5:00]

*Open a new browser tab. Navigate to backend.nutriaid.eu/admin.*

**VOICEOVER:**
"For operators and acquirers, NutriAID includes a fully featured admin console."

*Click Settings → AI Brain.*

"The AI Brain settings panel gives full control over the AI pipeline."

*Show the model selector, temperature slider, system prompt textarea.*

"Change the AI model, adjust the response temperature, add global instructions that apply to every worker..."

*Click on the Workers tab.*

"...or configure individual workers. Every worker can be enabled or disabled, and its prompt can be customised at runtime — without any code changes or server restarts."

*Navigate to AI Test Lab.*

"The AI Test Lab lets operators run, inspect, and debug the full pipeline."

*Click the Orchestrator tab. Run a test meal-plan request.*

"Here you can see every worker executing in sequence, their supervision reports, and — if any correction was applied — exactly what was changed and why."

*Navigate to Settings → Acquisition Downloads.*

"And the Acquisition Downloads panel tracks every document download from the portal — including IP address, country, user agent, and timestamp — with CSV export and per-document statistics."

---

### [SCENE 7 — ACQUISITION PORTAL — 5:00–5:30]

*Navigate to nutriaid.eu/acquire.*

**VOICEOVER:**
"Finally, the Acquisition Portal."

*Scroll through the hero, badge section, document sections.*

"Thirty-one documents covering every dimension of the platform — technical architecture, business valuation, legal pack, and media assets — all available for download with full tracking."

*Click on a document download button. Download begins.*

"Every download is logged, attributed, and visible to the operator in real time."

---

### [SCENE 8 — CLOSING TITLE CARD — 5:30–5:50]

*Black screen. NutriAID logo returns.*

**VOICEOVER:**
"NutriAID. A production-ready AI nutrition platform. Available for acquisition. €70,000–€120,000."

*Text appears: "Download the full acquisition document library at nutriaid.eu/acquire"*

*Fade to black.*
