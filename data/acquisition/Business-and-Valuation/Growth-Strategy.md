## Pillar 1: Organic Content and GEO

### Strategy

NutriAID is architecturally GEO-ready (Generative Engine Optimisation). The platform and its public content are structured for ingestion and citation by AI-powered search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews).

### Execution

| Activity | Timeline | Expected Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Publish 50+ intolerance-focused blog posts (EN + RO) | Month 1–3 | Organic search traffic + AI citation |
| Create free "Gluten-Free Meal Plan Generator" landing page | Month 1 | High-intent inbound traffic |
| YouTube / TikTok short-form content (symptom + food correlation) | Month 2–6 | Creator audience, brand awareness |
| SEO for "AI nutrition app Romania", "meal plan gluten free" | Month 1–12 | Consistent inbound |
| Build public recipe database (GEO-optimised, structured data) | Month 3–6 | Long-tail search + AI citations |

**CAC for organic:** ~€0 (content creation cost only)

## Pillar 3: B2B Partnerships

### Phase 1 — Dietitian Partnerships (Month 3–9)

Target 20 solo-practice dietitians in Romania. Offer:
- Free white-label trial (3 months)
- Co-marketing (dietitian's name + NutriAID branding)
- Revenue share on patient subscriptions referred

Expected conversion: 50% of trial partners → paid licence (€149–299/month).

### Phase 2 — Gastroenterology Clinics (Month 6–18)

Partner with 5–10 private gastroenterology clinics in Romania (Bucharest, Cluj, Timișoara).

**Sales approach:**
- Attend gastroenterology medical conferences
- Offer 60-day pilot with 10 test patients
- Demonstrate symptom-food correlation monitoring value

Expected ACV: €600–999/month per clinic.

### Phase 3 — CEE Expansion via Distributor (Month 12–24)

Partner with health app distributors or digital health agencies in Hungary, Poland, and Czech Republic to handle local sales. Revenue share: 20–30% of licence fees.

## Retention and Engagement Strategy

Long-term growth requires retention, not just acquisition.

| Tactic | Implementation |
|---|---|
| Push notifications for journal reminders | Already implemented (VAPID push) |
| Weekly AI-generated progress summary email | 2 weeks to implement |
| Streak rewards for daily journal entries | 1 week to implement |
| Community features (recipes, shared meal plans) | 6–8 weeks |
| Personalised onboarding sequence (email) | Already partially implemented |

**Target:** Reduce monthly churn from 6% (early) to 3.5% at 12 months, through journal habit formation.
