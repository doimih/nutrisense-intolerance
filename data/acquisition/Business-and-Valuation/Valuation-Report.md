NutriAID is offered for acquisition at a price range of **€70,000 – €120,000**. This valuation reflects the platform's current state as a fully operational, technically sophisticated, production-ready SaaS, with significant expansion potential across the EU nutrition and wellness market.

This report provides a multi-method valuation analysis supporting the asking price and contextualising the opportunity for prospective acquirers.

## Method 2: Revenue Multiple (Forward ARR)

This method applies a market multiple to the platform's projected Annual Recurring Revenue.

### Current Revenue Status

The platform is pre-revenue at the time of this report (in active development / pre-launch marketing phase). A conservative forward ARR is projected based on:

- 18-month trajectory to stabilised user acquisition
- Conservative churn assumptions (6%/month early, 4% at maturity)
- Blended ARPU of €10/month

### Forward ARR Scenarios

| Scenario | 12-Month Users | 12-Month ARR |
|---|---|---|
| Conservative | 500 paying | €60,000 |
| Base | 1,200 paying | €144,000 |

Early-stage SaaS platforms with strong technical foundations and high-growth markets typically trade at 0.5x – 2x forward ARR in acquisition transactions under €500K.

## Value Drivers Supporting Upper Range

The following factors justify valuation at the upper end of the €70,000–€120,000 range:

### 1. Technical Sophistication
The AI orchestration system, self-healing pipeline, and semantic validation layer represent significant intellectual property. No comparable micro-SaaS in this price range includes equivalent AI engineering depth.

### 2. Regulatory Readiness
The medical disclaimer system, GDPR-compliant architecture, and safety validation layer make NutriAID deployable in regulated EU healthcare-adjacent contexts without additional legal engineering.

### 3. White-Label Capability
The platform is immediately deployable under any brand. This multiplies the total addressable market — the acquirer can sell to clinics, wellness brands, and corporate buyers, not only end consumers.

### 4. GEO-Optimised Architecture
The platform is structured for AI-driven discovery (Generative Engine Optimisation). As LLM-powered search continues to grow, GEO-ready platforms capture traffic that traditional SEO misses.

### 5. Full i18n (RO + EN)
Day-one readiness for both Romanian and English markets, with AI workers that generate responses in the user's selected language.

### 6. Complete Admin Console
A fully featured admin panel (AI Brain configuration, user management, acquisition tracking, AI logs) means the acquirer can operate the platform without developer involvement for day-to-day management.
