## Pre-Sale Legal Checklist

Before finalising the transaction, verify:

- [ ] NDA signed by both parties
- [ ] Technical due diligence completed (buyer has reviewed the code)
- [ ] Financial due diligence completed (if revenue exists)
- [ ] IP Transfer Agreement or License Agreement signed
- [ ] Terms of Sale agreed and signed
- [ ] Liability Disclaimer signed by buyer
- [ ] Escrow method agreed (recommended: Escrow.com)
- [ ] User data transfer plan (if applicable, GDPR)
- [ ] Domain transfer initiated or planned
- [ ] Access credentials prepared for transfer
- [ ] Onboarding session scheduled
