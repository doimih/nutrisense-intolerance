## Phase 1: Organic Launch (Month 1–3)

**Target:** First 100 registered users, first 10 paying users
**Budget:** €0–200/month
**Primary channel:** SEO + social media

### 1.1 SEO — Primary Investment

**Why SEO first:**
- Zero marginal cost
- Compound returns (content ranked today generates traffic for 3+ years)
- Food intolerance keywords have low competition in Romanian

**Target keywords (Romanian):**
- "intoleranță la lactoză simptome" (6,600 searches/month, low competition)
- "intoleranță la gluten ce mânânci" (4,400/month)
- "journal alimentar online" (1,200/month)
- "ce alimente evit IBS" (880/month)
- "aplicație intoleranțe alimentare" (200/month — captures high-intent)

**Content plan (Knowledge Hub — already built):**
The platform already has 9 informational articles. Expand to 20 articles in 3 months:
- "Complete guide to lactose intolerance: symptoms, foods, alternatives"
- "FODMAP diet — what to eat and what to avoid"
- "IBS: how to identify food triggers in 30 days"
- "Gluten intolerance vs celiac disease — key differences"
- "Daily food journal: why it works for intolerance detection"

Each article targets a cluster of long-tail keywords and links to platform sign-up.

### 1.2 Social Media

**Platform:** Facebook + Instagram (primary for Romanian wellness market)

**Content types:**
- Before/after stories (user finds their trigger — anonymised, composite)
- Symptom tips ("Did you know bloating can appear 8h after eating?")
- Quick intolerance guides (lactose/gluten — carousel format)
- Platform screenshots + demos
- Polls ("Do you track your symptoms?")

**Posting frequency:** 3×/week minimum
**Cost:** €0 (organic) or €50–100/month (minor paid boost)

**Community:** Join and participate in Romanian groups:
- Intoleranță lactoza Romania (Facebook, 10,000+ members)
- Gluten free Romania (Facebook, 5,000+ members)
- IBS Support Romania

### 1.3 Influencer Micro-Partnerships

**Target:** Romanian wellness micro-influencers (5,000–50,000 followers)
- Nutrition and lifestyle YouTubers
- Dietitians on Instagram
- Wellness bloggers

**Approach:** Free Pro+ access for 3 months in exchange for honest review
**Cost:** €0 (platform cost only)
**Expected reach:** 5,000–50,000 per influencer

## Phase 2: Traction (Month 3–9)

**Target:** 500 registered users, 50 paying users, MRR €750
**Budget:** €200–800/month

### 2.1 Paid Acquisition

**Facebook/Instagram Ads:**
- Target: Romanian women 25–45 interested in healthy eating, digestive health, wellness
- Ad format: Problem → Solution ("Tired of not knowing what's causing your bloating?")
- CTA: Free 7-day trial
- Budget: €200–400/month
- Expected CAC: €15–30 (well within LTV of Pro/Pro+)

**Google Search Ads:**
- High-intent keywords: "food intolerance app", "lactose intolerance tracking"
- Budget: €100–200/month
- Expected CAC: €20–40

**Retargeting:**
- Facebook Pixel: retarget visitors who started registration but didn't complete
- Budget: €50/month
- Expected conversion lift: 30–50%

### 2.2 Referral Programme

**Mechanism:**
- Each user gets a unique link
- Successful referral = 1 free month for referrer + 14-day extended trial for referee
- Automatically tracked (extend trial logic already in the billing system)

**Expected impact:** Viral coefficient > 0.3 (each 10 users brings 3 more)

### 2.3 Dietitian Partnerships

**Approach:** Contact nutrition practices and offer:
- Free accounts for nutritionist + 5 patients
- Co-branded recommendation: "Our preferred digital companion for monitoring"
- Monthly fee for clinic account: €49–99/month

**Expected:** 10 clinics × 5 patients each = 50 new users/month at low CAC

### 2.4 Email Marketing

Build an email list via:
- Free symptom checklist PDF (lead magnet on landing page)
- Newsletter with weekly intolerance tips
- Onboarding sequence: 7 emails in 7 trial days (trial maximisation)

**Tool:** Brevo (free up to 9,000 emails/month)

## Phase 3: Scale (Month 9–18)

**Target:** 2,000+ registered users, 200+ paying, MRR €3,000–5,000
**Budget:** €1,000–3,000/month

### 3.1 Geographic Expansion — Eastern Europe

**Markets:** Poland, Hungary, Bulgaria, Czech Republic
**Required investment:** Translation of landing page + emails into local language
**AI** already handles EN requests; workers use EN prompts by default

**Strategy:**
- Localised landing pages targeting local keywords
- Partner with local wellness influencers
- Facebook campaigns in local language

### 3.2 B2B — White-Label Offer

**Target buyers:**
- Nutrition clinics (100–500 patients)
- Pharmacies with wellness departments
- Health insurance companies offering wellness benefits
- Corporate HR departments (employee wellness)

**White-label offer:**
- Complete rebranding: 1–4 hours of work
- Licence price: €1,500–5,000 one-time
- Monthly licence: €200–500/month
- Support: included in licence

**Sales approach:**
- Direct outreach to nutrition clinic owners via LinkedIn
- Presentation at medical/nutrition conferences (Romania, Poland)
- Partner with pharmacy chains (Dona, Help Net, Sensiblu in Romania)

### 3.3 App Store Presence

**React Native app** based on existing web UI:
- Estimated development: 200–400 hours
- App Store + Google Play = additional distribution channel
- Push notifications for daily journaling reminders (native integration)
- Barcode scan for food logging

### 3.4 Content Marketing — Advanced

**Podcast:**
- "Nutrition and intolerances" — Romanian/EN bilingual
- Guest episodes with nutritionists and gastroenterologists
- CTA: try NutriAID

**YouTube Channel:**
- "Intolerance diaries" format — weekly, real users
- Tutorial: how to use NutriAID
- Q&A with nutritionists

**Expected SEO compound effect by month 18:** 50,000+ monthly organic visitors

## Phase 4: Maturity (Month 18–36)

**Target:** €15,000+ MRR, profitability, geographic expansion
**Strategy:** Double down on what worked in Phase 3

### 4.1 Premium Features

**Add-ons for additional revenue:**
- "Medical Export" PDF: structured PDF for doctor visits (€5 per export)
- Video consultation with partner dietitian: €30–80/session (NutriAID as marketplace)
- Premium AI Coaching: daily 1-on-1 AI interaction with personalised meal planning (€15/month add-on)

### 4.2 API for Developers (B2B)

Open a paid API for integrating NutriAID's guidance engine:
- €0.05–0.15 per API call
- Potential integrators: fitness apps, health apps, wearable manufacturers
- Developer portal + documentation

### 4.3 Partnerships with Supermarkets / Food Brands

**Data collaboration (anonymised + aggregate):**
- Product reformulation insights for food brands
- "NutriAID approved" label for intolerance-friendly products
- Affiliate fees for recommending certified products
