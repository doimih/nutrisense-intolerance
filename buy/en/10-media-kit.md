## 4. Platform Key Features (for press releases)

### Feature 1: Multi-Worker AI Orchestrator
NutriAID's AI engine consists of 11 specialised workers — each with a distinct role: analysing user profiles, checking intolerances, building meal plans, calculating nutrition, and validating medical safety. Unlike generic AI chatbots, each recommendation is the product of a multi-stage pipeline with integrated validation.

### Feature 2: Self-Healing Architecture
If the primary AI model (GPT-4o) is unavailable, the platform automatically switches to Gemini 1.5 Pro. If both fail, a rule-based engine generates responses at zero cost. Users always receive an answer.

### Feature 3: Medical Safety Validation
Every AI response passes through a dedicated semantic validator before reaching the user. Any diagnosis, prescription, or absolute medical claim is automatically removed. NutriAID is designed to be a nutrition guide, not a medical diagnostic tool.

### Feature 4: Zero-Code Admin Console
Operators can configure the entire platform — AI models, Stripe products, email settings, pricing, user plans — without touching the code. The admin console includes an AI Test Lab for live testing of prompt changes.

### Feature 5: Full GDPR Compliance
Users can export all their data (JSON + PDF) or permanently delete their account and all associated data from the platform, in compliance with EU data protection regulation.

## 6. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ for media)

**Q: Is NutriAID a medical device?**
A: No. NutriAID is a nutrition guidance platform. It does not diagnose conditions, prescribe treatments, or replace the advice of a doctor or dietitian. Its medical safety validator ensures no medical claims reach users.

**Q: Does it actually use real AI or just OpenAI wrapper?**
A: NutriAID features a proprietary 11-worker orchestrator. OpenAI (GPT-4o) is one of the AI providers used, but the orchestration logic, worker chains, validation, and auto-correction are proprietary. The AI provider can be swapped (to Gemini, Claude, or local models) without changing the product's core logic.

**Q: Is the platform available internationally?**
A: Yes. Full English support is implemented. The platform is currently deployed primarily for the Romanian market, with English as the second language ready for international expansion.

**Q: What data does NutriAID collect?**
A: Name, email, physical data (optional: age, height, weight, activity level), food journal entries (meals, symptoms, notes), and usage data. All data is stored on EU infrastructure. Users can export or delete all their data at any time.

**Q: Who is the technical team behind NutriAID?**
A: NutriAID was built by a small, experienced development team specialising in full-stack web applications, AI integrations, and SaaS product development. The platform is now available for acquisition to allow the team to focus on new projects.
